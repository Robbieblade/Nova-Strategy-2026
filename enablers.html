Our Enablers

These are not strategic priorities. They are the systems that make the strategy possible. If any enabler fails, the whole strategy is at risk.

Eight foundations

Without these, no pillar stands

The three pillars — Teamwork, Innovation, Results — are how Nova makes progress. The enablers are how Nova stays standing. They are maintained, invested in and protected so that the strategy can do its work.

1

People

We recruit, develop and retain exceptional colleagues across every role.

  • We attract people who share our values and are committed to transforming the lives of our young people.
  • We attract people who share our values and are committed to transforming lives.
  • We develop every colleague through clear pathways, honest conversations and genuine investment in their growth.
  • We retain our best people by making Nova a place where talented individuals want to build a career.
2

Governance

We provide the challenge, support and strategic direction that excellent governance requires.

  • Trustees and governors hold the trust and its schools to account, asking the right questions with access to the right information.
  • Every school has a local governing board that knows its community, understands its context and supports its leadership.
  • Governance is developmental — it builds the capacity of leaders as well as holding them to account.
3

Finance

We ensure the long-term financial sustainability of the trust and every school within it.

  • Every school targets a balanced budget, making prudent decisions about staffing, resources and expenditure.
  • Central procurement, shared services and financial controls reduce waste and improve value for money.
  • Financial health enables ambition — we invest in the things that improve outcomes for children.
4

Infrastructure

We create safe, effective and future-ready environments in which children and adults can thrive.

  • Every building is maintained to a standard that communicates care, ambition and respect.
  • Therapeutic classroom design, Thrive provision and enhanced learning environments are infrastructure investments that directly support our strategy.
  • We plan our estate with the long term in mind, making decisions that will benefit future generations of children.
5

Compliance

We meet and exceed our statutory responsibilities, ensuring every child is safe and every school is well-managed.

  • Safeguarding is the foundation of everything. The safety and wellbeing of children guide every decision we take.
  • We meet our duties as an employer and a public body, consistently and without complacency.
  • We embrace high standards, going beyond statutory requirements and continually developing our practice to lead the sector in how we discharge our responsibilities.
6

Technology

We use technology to amplify learning, improve workflow and connect our schools and teams.

  • Our digital strategy is purposeful — technology is used because it improves outcomes, not for its own sake.
  • Microsoft Copilot, Showbie, Magma Maths, Quest Progress, Reading Progress and Kaligo are tools in service of learning and efficiency, not ends in themselves.
  • We invest in strong cyber security to protect learners, staff and data, and to keep schools running without disruption.
  • We govern AI use responsibly — encouraging confident, safe adoption rather than prohibition or uncritical use.
7

Data

We use data intelligently to inform strategy, support decisions and measure what matters.

  • Our data lake and data analysis tools give us comparable, trustworthy data across every school, so leaders act on evidence rather than instinct.
  • Data informs strategy and decision-making at every level, from the classroom to the board.
  • We measure success against clear benchmarks, tracking progress toward our priorities and holding ourselves to account.
8

Transforming Lives Foundation

The charitable arm of Nova Education Trust, unlocking additional opportunities for disadvantaged children and communities beyond what schools can provide alone.

  • Unlocks access to enrichment, experiences and networks that broaden horizons and raise aspiration.
  • Aligns partnerships, investment, funding and social value with Trust priorities, bringing in additional resource that schools cannot generate independently.
  • Converts the Trust's USP promises into real, lived experiences for pupils.
The bigger picture

Strategy and enablers together

The three pillars are what Nova is working towards. The eight enablers are what keeps Nova capable of doing that work. Leaders at every level are responsible for both — the strategy and the systems that sustain it.
