What success looks like

For pupils

  • Attend regularly
  • Belong to their school community

For staff

  • Trust leaders and feel supported
  • Develop professionally
  • Work collaboratively
  • Recommend Nova as a place to work

For schools

  • Share expertise openly
  • Support one another
  • Collaborate effectively
  • Operate as one family

The three-year roadmap

Aligning culture, attendance and belonging

Build the foundations, embed them, then sharpen and stretch.

Year 12026–27 · Align & establish
  • Embed the trust-wide Attendance Strategy and Behaviour Framework in every school — primary at or above national, secondary closing the gap.
  • Run the first trust-wide staff engagement survey to baseline retention and the ‘recommend Nova’ measure.
  • Establish a single inclusion standard across schools, with SENCOs on every leadership team.
Year 22027–28 · Embed & connect
  • Attendance above national in primary, within reach in secondary; persistent absence falling.
  • Retention moving toward top quartile; engagement improving year on year.
  • Belonging and parent satisfaction surveys running in every school with comparable data.
Year 32028–30 · Sharpen & stretch
  • Attendance above national in every phase; retention in the top quartile; over 90% recommending Nova.
  • Inclusion and belonging above national benchmarks; collaboration between schools evident everywhere.
Where this sits
