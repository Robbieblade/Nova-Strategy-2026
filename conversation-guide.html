Growth & Development Conversation Guide

Three conversations. Every role. One purpose.

Appraisal at Nova isn’t a form-filling exercise. It’s three honest conversations built on the same three pillars, tailored to each role. These questions guide the discussion.

01

Teamwork

How do you strengthen the people and systems around you?

02

Innovation

How do you improve your practice and our organisation?

03

Results

What impact are you having on children, colleagues and schools?

One shared purpose: we are stronger together. Great schools are built by every role.
Tailored prompts by role

The same three pillars, in the language of your role

Executive Team

CEO, CFO, Directors, Trust Leaders
Teamwork
  • How do you build trust and alignment across the Trust?
  • How do you develop and empower school leaders?
  • How do you create the conditions for staff to thrive?
Innovation
  • What strategic improvements have you led?
  • How are you driving innovation and system change?
  • How are you developing leadership capacity?
  • What do you need to learn or explore more deeply?
Results
  • What impact are we having across our schools?
  • What outcomes demonstrate our strategic priorities?
  • How are we improving life chances for children and communities?

School Leaders

Headteachers, Principals, SLT, Assistant Leaders
Teamwork
  • How do you build a positive, high-trust culture?
  • How do you develop your staff?
  • How do you promote inclusion and wellbeing?
Innovation
  • How are you improving teaching and learning?
  • How are you using evidence and data to improve?
  • How do you develop others through coaching?
  • What innovation or improvement have you led?
Results
  • How are your pupils progressing and achieving?
  • What impact are you having on attendance and behaviour?
  • How are you closing gaps and improving outcomes?
  • What are your key priorities for improvement?

Teachers

Teamwork
  • How do you build positive relationships with pupils?
  • How do you collaborate and support colleagues?
  • How do you create an inclusive classroom?
Innovation
  • How are you developing your teaching practice?
  • How do you use research or new strategies?
  • How do you share ideas or support others?
  • What development will have the biggest impact?
Results
  • How are your pupils learning and progressing?
  • What impact do your lessons have on outcomes?
  • How do you know your teaching makes a difference?
  • What are your priorities for your pupils?

Teaching Assistants

Teamwork
  • How do you support pupils and your teacher?
  • How do you build positive relationships?
  • How do you help create a calm, positive environment?
Innovation
  • How are you developing your skills and knowledge?
  • What new strategies or ideas have you tried?
  • How do you support pupil independence and learning?
  • What training or support will help you grow?
Results
  • How do you impact the learning and progress of pupils?
  • What difference do you make to individual pupils?
  • How do you know your support is effective?
  • What are your goals for the year ahead?

Central Team

Finance, HR, Health & Safety, IT, Communications etc.
Teamwork
  • How do you work collaboratively with schools and teams?
  • How do you provide excellent service and support?
  • How do you represent Nova’s values in your work?
Innovation
  • What efficiencies or improvements have you introduced?
  • How are you using technology to add value?
  • What skills or knowledge will help you do more?
Results
  • How does your work support school improvement?
  • What impact do you have across the Trust?
  • How do you measure the effectiveness of your service?
  • What are your priorities for continuous improvement?

Site & Operations

Site Managers, Cleaners, Catering, Maintenance etc.
Teamwork
  • How do you help create a safe, welcoming environment?
  • How do you support staff, pupils and visitors?
  • How do you take pride in your contribution?
Innovation
  • How are you improving routines or processes?
  • What ideas have you suggested to make things better?
  • How do you keep learning and developing?
Results
  • How does your work keep our schools running well?
  • What impact do you have on safety and environment?
  • What do others say about your contribution?
  • What are your goals for the year ahead?
We grow together

Listen. Reflect. Develop. Achieve.

Listen Create space for open, honest dialogue.
Reflect Recognise strengths and celebrate impact.
Develop Plan next steps for growth and learning.

Every conversation is an opportunity to learn, improve and make a difference.
