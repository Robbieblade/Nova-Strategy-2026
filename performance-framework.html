The Nova Performance Framework

Everyone. Every role. One purpose.

A simple approach that values how we work together, how we improve, and the impact we make. The three pillars aren't just a trust-level story — they're how every person at Nova understands their contribution, is developed, and is recognised.

01

Teamwork

How do you strengthen the people and systems around you?

Work hard, be kind. Are you reliable and trusted? Do you collaborate, support others and contribute under pressure?
02

Innovation

How do you improve the way the school works?

Do you look for better ways to do things? Simplify and reduce friction? Solve problems creatively and share ideas?
03

Results

What tangible impact do you have in your role?

What impact do you have on pupils, staff or the school? Do you deliver high-quality work consistently?
One shared purpose: we are stronger together. Great schools are built by every role.

How we assess contribution

Three sources, weighted toward what people actually do

1

Manager observations

Day-to-day impact, professional judgement and evidence.

2

Wider feedback

Thoughtful input from the colleagues who work with the person.

3

Contribution evidence

Concrete examples of impact, initiative and support for others.

The starting point is strengths. We recognise what people do well and direct development where it will have the biggest impact — not a deficit checklist.

Why this matters

A shared language, a stronger culture, better outcomes

Shared language A common approach for every role.
Stronger culture Built on trust, respect and support.
Better outcomes For our pupils, our staff and our community.

This isn't sentiment. Apple built its strategy retreats on the principle that the best ideas come from anywhere in the organisation, not just the top — the people in the room were chosen by their importance to the future, not their rank. Nova holds the same belief. A site manager's idea for a calmer environment, or a TA's strategy that unlocks a child's independence, is innovation in exactly the sense the Trust means it.

Every role has all three questions to answer.
